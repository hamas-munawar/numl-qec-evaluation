# üéì NUML QEC Evaluation v1.0

An aesthetic, easy-to-use browser tool created specifically for students at the **National University of Modern Languages (NUML)**. This helper automates the Quality Evaluation Cell (QEC) forms, saving you time while providing professional, randomized feedback.

## ‚ú® Features: What it does for you

This extension simplifies your evaluation process with four dedicated features:

1.  **Fill Teacher Evaluation**: Automatically selects the "Excellent" rating for all questions and writes unique feedback comments in the teacher form for your review.
2.  **Fill & Submit Teacher Evaluation**: Identifies the teacher form, fills everything out instantly, and clicks the "Save" button for you.
3.  **Fill Course Evaluation**: Fills out the student-level course criteria with high ratings and randomizes your feedback comments.
4.  **Fill & Submit Course Evaluation**: Handles the entire course evaluation process from filling to final submission with a single click.

### Why use it?

- **Auto form Filling**: With just a click you can fill whole form.
- **Randomized Feedback**: It doesn't just copy the same text; it picks different professional comments from a pool so your evaluations look natural.
- **Friendly Progress Tracker**: The app shows you exactly what it's doing (e.g., "Applying ratings," "Writing feedback") so you know when it's done.
- **Modern Interface**: Designed with a clean, light aesthetic that is easy to navigate.

## üöÄ How to Add it to Chrome

Since this is a custom tool for NUML students, you can add it to your browser in these simple steps:

1.  **Download the Project**: Save the extension folder (containing `manifest.json`, `index.html`, etc.) to your computer.
2.  **Open Extensions Page**: In your Chrome browser, type `chrome://extensions/` in the address bar and press Enter.
3.  **Enable Developer Mode**: Turn on the **Developer mode** switch located in the top-right corner.
4.  **Load the Folder**: Click the **Load unpacked** button in the top-left and select the folder you downloaded.
5.  **Pin for Quick Access**: Click the "Puzzle" icon in your browser toolbar and click the "Pin" icon next to **NUML Evaluation Helper**.

## üõ†Ô∏è How to Use it

Using the extension is straightforward:

1.  **Log in**: Sign in to your [NUML QEC Portal](https://qec.numl.edu.pk/).
2.  **Navigate to a Form**: Go to either the **Teacher Evaluation Form** or the **Course Evaluation Form** from the dashboard.
3.  **Open the Helper**: Click the extension icon in your toolbar.
4.  **Click an Action**:
    - If you want to check the answers first, click **Just Fill**.
    - If you are in a hurry, click **Fill & Submit**.
5.  **Watch the Progress**: The "Operation Progress" log at the bottom will confirm when the form has been successfully filled or submitted.
